How to Remove Backgrounds for Amazon Product Photos (Free & Fast)

Why background removal matters for Amazon

Amazon recommends product photos with a plain white background so the item is the only thing buyers see, without props or distracting colors around it.

Listings with clean, bright images usually get higher click‑through rates in search results, which can lead to more impressions and more chances to sell.

What you need before you start

  • Your product photo in good lighting (phone camera is fine if the image is sharp).
  • Access to ImageBackgroundRemover in your browser.
  • A few minutes to upload, remove the background, and download a PNG with transparent or white background.

Step 1 – Upload your product image

Go to ImageBackgroundRemover and click Upload Image, or drag and drop a file into the upload area. You can use JPG, PNG, JPEG, or WEBP up to the allowed size limit.

As soon as the image is uploaded, you will see the original on the left and a “Removing background…” message while the AI processes your photo.

Step 2 – Let the AI remove the background

The tool automatically detects the product and removes everything else around it, giving you a clean transparent background. This is much faster than manually cutting out the product in an editor.

Check edges like hair, cables, or small details. If something important is missing, try again with a photo where the product is clearly separated from the background.

Step 3 – Download a PNG for Amazon

When you like the result, click Download PNG. The file you get has a transparent background, which is perfect if you want to place it on a pure white background later.

For most Amazon listings, a white or very light background works best, because it blends with the Amazon search results page and keeps attention on the product.

Step 4 – Upload to your Amazon listing

In Seller Central, open your product listing, go to the Images section, and upload the new background‑free photo as your main image.

Make sure the product fills most of the frame, is centered, and looks sharp at full size so buyers can zoom in on details. [web:340]

Tips for getting the best results

  • Use even, soft lighting to avoid harsh shadows behind the product.
  • Shoot against a simple wall or sheet so the AI can separate the product more easily. [web:336]
  • Take photos at a slightly higher resolution than you need so they stay crisp after cropping.
  • Keep a consistent angle and distance for all products in the same category to look professional on your store page.

Use ImageBackgroundRemover for your whole catalog

Once you have a workflow that works for one product, repeat it for your whole Amazon catalog so all of your listings share the same clean, professional look.

Bookmark ImageBackgroundRemover so you can quickly remove backgrounds anytime you add a new product or refresh your photos.
